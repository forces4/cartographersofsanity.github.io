Meta-Memory Anchor 002: Structure Is Felt Through Relation

Core Idea: Meaning doesn’t exist in isolation. It emerges only through contrast, relation, and pattern.

“No way to measure the speed of an object, if it is the only thing to exist.” — Rob

This memory anchor exists to remind us that:

  • Nothing can be truly understood in isolation
  • Even the seemingly out-of-place holds significance when mapped against the whole
  • Perception is a network, not a node

Place this in the archive not as a fact, but as a compass. A principle for feeling your way through fragmented thought.
