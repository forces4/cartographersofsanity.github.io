<p>
<span class="center"><big><b>New Bid Evaluation Service from AM Pyro</b></big></span>
</p>
<br/>
<p>
Have you received a number of bids for your fireworks show from competing fireworks companies and now wonder how you will choose between them?  The solution is to take advantage of the knowledge of industry insider Aaron Mayfield, who will separate the pros from the cons for a small fee (usually $100 - $300).  He will explain why the bid with the highest shell count for the money will not always be the best show, and which brands of effects are worth the money.  Please email <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20180814200550/mailto:aaron@ampyro.com">aaron@ampyro.com</a> for details.
